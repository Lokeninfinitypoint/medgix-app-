Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

The 7-day free trial gives you complete access to all 3,296+ tools, AI agents, automation features, and analytics. No credit card required, and you can cancel anytime.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any payments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for enterprise plans.

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
