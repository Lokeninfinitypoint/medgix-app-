9 strategies to promote your business on Instagram

Your Instagram profile is your digital storefront and first impressions matter. To make the most of this valuable real estate:

Choose a recognizable profile picture. Use your logo or a high-quality image that represents your brand.

Craft a compelling bio. In 150 characters or less, tell visitors who you are and what you offer. Include a call to action (CTA) and relevant emojis to make it eye-catching. Fashion Nova, for example, managed to give you shipping details, announce new products, ask for user-generated content, and offer a discount code, all in one short bio.

Utilize the link in the bio. You should direct users to your website, the latest product, or a landing page with multiple links. Look at how Converse uses the link to direct you to their limited edition sneaker on their website.

Mix up your content types. Take full advantage of Instagram's various formats like Feed posts, Stories, and Reels to keep your content fresh and engaging.¬†

6. Use hashtags strategically

Hashtags are still a crucial part of Instagram's discovery system.

  • Mix popular and niche hashtags. Use a combination to increase your chances of being discovered.
  • Don't overdo it. While you can use up to 30 hashtags per post, 5-9 well-chosen ones are often more effective.
  • Hide hashtags in the first comment. Hiding them keeps your captions clean while still benefiting from hashtag discoverability.

How do you promote on Instagram? FAQ

Check out these frequently asked questions to get all the answers you need.

How to promote a post on Instagram?

Promoting a post on Instagram is a straightforward process:

1. Ensure you have a business or creator account.

2. Go to the post you want to promote and tap ‚ÄòBoost‚Äô below the image.

3. Choose your goal (more profile visits, website visits, or messages).

4. Select your target audience or create a new one.

5. Set your budget and duration for the promotion.

6. Review your promotion and tap ‚ÄòCreate Promotion‚Äô when ready.

Is it free to promote on Instagram?

While creating and maintaining an Instagram account is free, promoting posts or running ads does come with a cost. However, Instagram offers flexible budgeting options, allowing you to spend as little as $1 per day on promotions. This flexibility makes it accessible for businesses of all sizes to start advertising on the platform.

How much does it cost to promote on Instagram?

The cost of promoting on Instagram varies widely depending on several factors, like your target audience, the type of ad you're running, your bidding strategy, and the time of year (costs can increase during peak shopping seasons).

Studies show that the average Instagram CPM is about $10.07 in August 2024, while the average CPC comes in at $1.12.¬†

In terms of the cost per engagement (CPE), you‚Äôre looking at around $0.059.

Remember, these are just averages, and your actual costs may differ. It's best to start with a small budget and adjust based on performance.

Why can't I boost my Instagram posts?

If you're having trouble boosting your Instagram posts, there could be several reasons:

1. Your account must be set up as a business or creator account.

2. Your post doesn't comply with Instagram's advertising policies and best practices.

3. Your Instagram page or post contains content that's not eligible for promotion (e.g., political content, alcohol, etc.).

4. You must still set up your payment method in the Facebook Ads Manager.

5. Your account is still too new or has violated Instagram's community guidelines in the past.

If you still have issues, check Instagram's help center or contact their support team.
