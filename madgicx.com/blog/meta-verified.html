Meta Verified FAQs

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about Meta Verified.

How much does Meta Verified cost?

Meta Verified has four packages for brands and businesses to suit wherever you are in your business growth trajectory. Subscription costs are:

  • Business Standard: $13.99/month or $143.99/year¬†
  • Business Plus: $44.99/month or $479.99/year

  • Business Max: $479.99/month or $4199.99/year

As they roll out the new packages across regions, all existing Meta Verified subscribers will automatically shift to the Meta Business Standard package.¬†

Meta Verified for personal Facebook or Instagram profiles, called a creator subscription, costs $14.99 monthly on mobile or $11.99 via the web version.¬†

This subscription cost is for a single profile (either Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp), but you save $1 if you opt for their two-profile subscription bundle.
