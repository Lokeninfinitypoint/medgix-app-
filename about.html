About Marketing Tool Pro

We're on a mission to revolutionize digital marketing through AI-powered automation, making advanced marketing tools accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Marketing Tool Pro by Infinity Solutions emerged from a simple observation: businesses were struggling to manage the complexity of modern digital marketing across multiple platforms.

We set out to build a unified platform that leverages artificial intelligence to automate and optimize marketing campaigns, making it easier for businesses to achieve their growth objectives without needing a team of specialists.

Today, we're proud to serve over 10,000 businesses worldwide, managing over $500M in annual ad spend and helping our customers achieve an average 300% increase in ROI.
